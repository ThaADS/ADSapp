# Video 2: Quick Start - Your First WhatsApp Message in 5 Minutes

**Duration**: 5-6 minutes
**Target Audience**: New users completing initial setup
**Learning Objectives**:
- Successfully send first WhatsApp message through ADSapp
- Understand basic inbox navigation
- Learn fundamental message operations

---

## Introduction (0:00-0:20)

**NARRATION**: "Ready to send your first WhatsApp message through ADSapp? In the next five minutes, I'll walk you through everything you need to know to start having professional conversations with your customers. Let's dive right in."

**SCREEN ACTION**: Quick zoom into ADSapp dashboard from login screen

**CALLOUT**: "From Zero to First Message: 5 Minutes"

---

## Section 1: Accessing Your Inbox (0:20-0:50)

**NARRATION**: "First, log into your ADSapp dashboard. You'll see your inbox front and center. This is where all your WhatsApp conversations will appear. Think of it as your mission control for customer communication."

**SCREEN ACTION**:
- Show login page
- Transition to dashboard
- Highlight inbox area with subtle animation

**CALLOUT**: Point to navigation menu: "Inbox - Your Command Center"

**NARRATION CONTINUES**: "On the left, you'll see your conversation list. On the right, your message area. And up top, your search and filter tools. Everything is designed for speed and efficiency."

---

## Section 2: Understanding the Inbox Layout (0:50-1:40)

**NARRATION**: "Let's understand what you're looking at. Each conversation shows the customer's name, their last message, and a timestamp. You can see at a glance which conversations need attention."

**SCREEN ACTION**: Mouse cursor hovers over conversation items, highlighting each element

**CALLOUT**: Label key elements:
- Customer avatar/initials
- Name and phone number
- Last message preview
- Timestamp
- Unread indicator (blue dot)
- Assignment status

**NARRATION CONTINUES**: "The blue dot means unread messages. The assigned agent shows who's responsible. And these status tags help you organize conversations by stage - open, pending, or resolved."

**SCREEN ACTION**: Click through different status filters to demonstrate

**CALLOUT**: Show status options: "Open | Pending | Resolved"

---

## Section 3: Starting Your First Conversation (1:40-2:30)

**NARRATION**: "Now let's send your first message. Click the 'New Conversation' button in the top right. You'll need to enter the customer's WhatsApp number. Make sure to include the country code."

**SCREEN ACTION**:
- Click "New Conversation" button
- Show phone number input field with example format
- Type in example number: +1 555 0123

**CALLOUT**: "Format: +[Country Code][Phone Number]"
**EXAMPLE SHOWN**: "+1 555 0123"

**NARRATION CONTINUES**: "ADSapp will check if this number exists on WhatsApp and then open a conversation window. Now you're ready to type your first message."

**SCREEN ACTION**: Conversation window opens, cursor in message field

---

## Section 4: Composing and Sending Messages (2:30-3:30)

**NARRATION**: "Composing messages in ADSapp is intuitive. Type your message in the text field at the bottom. You can use formatting like bold, italic, or strikethrough using standard WhatsApp syntax."

**SCREEN ACTION**: Type example message with formatting:
"Hello! *Welcome to ADSapp.* We're here to help with _any questions_ you have."

**CALLOUT**: Show formatting shortcuts:
- *bold* = Bold
- _italic_ = Italic
- ~strikethrough~ = Strikethrough

**NARRATION CONTINUES**: "You can also add emojis, attach files, or send images by clicking these buttons. For now, let's keep it simple and just send a text message."

**SCREEN ACTION**: Briefly hover over attachment buttons, then focus on send

**NARRATION CONTINUES**: "When you're ready, click the send button or press Enter. Your message is now on its way through WhatsApp's official Business API."

**SCREEN ACTION**: Click send, show message appearing in conversation with delivery checkmarks

**CALLOUT**: Point to checkmarks: "✓ Sent | ✓✓ Delivered | ✓✓ Read (blue)"

---

## Section 5: Receiving and Responding (3:30-4:20)

**NARRATION**: "When a customer replies, you'll see their message appear in real-time. The conversation moves to the top of your inbox, and you'll hear a notification sound if you have those enabled."

**SCREEN ACTION**: Simulate incoming message appearing, conversation jumping to top

**CALLOUT**: Highlight unread indicator and notification badge

**NARRATION CONTINUES**: "To respond, simply type in the message field again. It's that straightforward. Notice how ADSapp shows you exactly when messages were sent, delivered, and read. This transparency helps you provide timely service."

**SCREEN ACTION**: Type and send a response:
"Thanks for reaching out! I can help you with that right away."

**NARRATION CONTINUES**: "You can scroll up to see conversation history, search within messages, and even download the entire conversation if needed."

**SCREEN ACTION**: Demonstrate scroll, show search icon

---

## Section 6: Using Quick Replies (4:20-5:00)

**NARRATION**: "Here's a pro tip to save time: use quick replies. Type a forward slash to see your saved responses. These are pre-written messages you can insert instantly."

**SCREEN ACTION**: Type "/" in message field, dropdown appears with quick replies

**CALLOUT**: Show quick reply menu with examples:
- /greeting - "Hello! How can I help you today?"
- /hours - "We're available Monday-Friday 9AM-6PM"
- /thanks - "Thank you for contacting us!"

**NARRATION CONTINUES**: "Select one, customize it if needed, and send. You've just saved yourself time while maintaining professional communication. We'll show you how to create your own quick replies in a later video."

**SCREEN ACTION**: Select a quick reply, shows in message field, send

---

## Section 7: Closing the Conversation (5:00-5:30)

**NARRATION**: "Once your conversation is complete, you can change its status to 'Resolved.' This keeps your inbox organized and helps you focus on active conversations."

**SCREEN ACTION**: Click status dropdown at top of conversation

**CALLOUT**: Show status options highlighted

**NARRATION CONTINUES**: "Don't worry, resolved conversations aren't deleted. They're just moved out of your active inbox. You can always search and find them later if the customer reaches out again."

**SCREEN ACTION**: Select "Resolved," conversation moves from inbox with animation

---

## Conclusion (5:30-6:00)

**NARRATION**: "Congratulations! You've just sent your first WhatsApp message through ADSapp. You now know how to navigate the inbox, start conversations, send messages, respond to customers, and keep your workspace organized."

**SCREEN ACTION**: Quick recap montage:
- Inbox overview
- New conversation
- Send message
- Receive response
- Use quick reply
- Mark resolved

**CALLOUT**: "✓ Inbox Navigation | ✓ First Message Sent | ✓ Professional Communication"

**NARRATION CONTINUES**: "In the next video, we'll give you a complete tour of the dashboard and all its powerful features. See you there!"

**CTA TEXT ON SCREEN**:
- "Next: Dashboard Navigation and Key Features"
- "Practice: Send 3 messages to build confidence"
- "Help: Visit docs.adsapp.com/getting-started"

## Production Notes

**Voice Tone**: Encouraging and instructional, patient pace
**Pacing**: Slower on technical steps, brief pauses after actions
**Music**: Light, unobtrusive background track
**Transitions**: Quick cuts for screen changes, no lengthy transitions
**Graphics**: Clear labels and arrows pointing to UI elements

**Key Visuals Needed**:
- Clean dashboard recording with no real customer data
- Example messages with professional content
- UI element highlights (buttons, fields, menus)
- Status indicators and their meanings
- Quick reply menu demonstration
- Checkmark states for message delivery

**Accessibility**:
- Caption all narration and on-screen text
- Describe all visual actions verbally
- Use high contrast for pointer/highlights
- Provide keyboard shortcut equivalents

**Recording Requirements**:
- Use test account with prepared data
- Show realistic but generic customer names
- Clear mouse movements, not too fast
- Multiple takes of each UI interaction for editing flexibility

**Demo Data Setup**:
- Pre-configure 3-5 sample conversations
- Create 3-4 quick reply templates
- Set up test WhatsApp number for receiving messages
- Prepare professional sample message content
