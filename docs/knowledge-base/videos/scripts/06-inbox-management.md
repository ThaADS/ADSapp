# Video 6: Inbox Management - Handling Conversations Efficiently

**Duration**: 6-8 minutes
**Target Audience**: Agents and team members handling daily conversations
**Learning Objectives**:
- Master inbox workflow for efficient conversation handling
- Learn conversation assignment and status management
- Use tags, filters, and search effectively

---

## Introduction (0:00-0:25)

**NARRATION**: "Your inbox is where customer relationships are built. In this video, I'll show you proven workflows for handling conversations efficiently, keeping your team organized, and ensuring no customer falls through the cracks."

**SCREEN ACTION**: Inbox view with multiple conversations, smooth navigation demonstration

**CALLOUT**: "Professional Inbox Management | Zero Missed Conversations"

---

## Section 1: Inbox Organization System (0:25-1:30)

**NARRATION**: "ADSapp's inbox uses a three-tier organization system: Status, Assignment, and Tags."

**SCREEN ACTION**: Visual diagram showing three organizational layers

**CALLOUT**: "Three-Tier Organization: Status | Assignment | Tags"

**NARRATION CONTINUES**: "Every conversation has a status: Open for active chats, Pending when waiting, and Resolved when complete. Conversations can be assigned to specific team members. And tags let you categorize by type, priority, or product line."

**SCREEN ACTION**: Show status filter with counts, assignments, tag examples

---

## Section 2: Processing New Conversations (1:30-3:00)

**NARRATION**: "Let's walk through handling a new conversation from start to finish."

**SCREEN ACTION**: New conversation appears with unread indicator

**CALLOUT**: "5-Step Workflow: Claim → Review Context → Respond → Tag → Update Status"

**NARRATION CONTINUES**: "First, assign it to yourself. Second, review the customer details panel for context. Third, respond using templates if appropriate. Fourth, add relevant tags. Finally, set the correct status."

**SCREEN ACTION**: Demonstrate each step smoothly with real examples

---

## Section 3: High-Volume Strategies (3:00-4:00)

**NARRATION**: "When your inbox is busy, efficiency becomes critical."

**SCREEN ACTION**: Show busy inbox with 50+ conversations

**CALLOUT**: "High-Volume Strategies: Triage → Batch → Shortcuts → Set Goals"

**NARRATION CONTINUES**: "Start by triaging urgent conversations. Batch similar requests together. Master keyboard shortcuts. And set realistic response time goals."

**SCREEN ACTION**: Demonstrate each strategy with metrics overlay

---

## Section 4: Team Collaboration (4:00-5:00)

**NARRATION**: "Collaboration features ensure seamless teamwork."

**SCREEN ACTION**: Show internal notes and mentions

**CALLOUT**: "Collaboration Tools: Internal Notes | @ Mentions | Conversation Transfer"

**NARRATION CONTINUES**: "Add internal notes only your team sees. Mention teammates with @ to notify them. Transfer conversations to specialists when needed."

**SCREEN ACTION**: Demonstrate collaboration workflow

## Section 5: Filters and Search (5:00-6:00)

**NARRATION**: "Powerful filtering helps you focus on what matters."

**SCREEN ACTION**: Demonstrate various filter combinations

**CALLOUT**: "Filter by: Status | Assignment | Tags | Date | Search Terms"

**NARRATION CONTINUES**: "Combine filters to find exactly what you need. Search across all message content, names, and phone numbers."
