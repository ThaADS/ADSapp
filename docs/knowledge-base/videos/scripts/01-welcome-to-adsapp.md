## Production Notes

**Voice Tone**: Enthusiastic but professional, friendly and welcoming
**Pacing**: Moderate with clear pauses between sections
**Music**: Upbeat, modern corporate background track at low volume
**Transitions**: Smooth fade or slide transitions between sections
**Graphics**: Clean, professional animations aligned with brand colors

**Key Visuals Needed**:

- ADSapp logo animation (opening)
- Dashboard screenshots (multiple angles)
- Feature icons (unified inbox, collaboration, automation, templates, analytics)
- Industry use case visuals
- Getting started workflow diagram
- Next video preview thumbnails

**Accessibility**:

- Full captions for all narration
- High contrast text overlays
- Clear visual indicators for all features mentioned
- Descriptive alt text for graphics

**Recording Requirements**:

- 1920x1080 resolution
- Screen recordings at 60fps
- Voiceover recorded in quiet environment
- Background music separate audio track for easy mixing
